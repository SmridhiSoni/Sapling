About Vines and Creepers


A vine is any plant with a growth habit of trailing or scandent (that is, climbing) stems, lianas or runners. The word vine can also refer to such stems or runners themselves, for instance, when used in wicker work.


With their sweet scents and lovely blooms, vines are something every garden needs to round out the design. Some vines are annual, so they last one season. Others return year after year, so they're considered perennial.
