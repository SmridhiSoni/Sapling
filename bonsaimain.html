About Bonsai


Bonsai is an ancient Japanese art form which was given great importance. The term itself means “planted in a container”. These adorable and tiny plants mimic the appearance of fully grown trees while being a fraction of the size. They provide a zen feel and symbolize peace, harmony and balance. Also with its immense significance growing and taking care of a bonsai is treated as a hobby by many and is enjoyed by people of all ages. These plants also look great in many different settings and are amusing to look at.
