Description


Agave Celsii 'Multicolour' is a evergreen succulent forming rosettes to 2 ft_ wide. Bold and attractive clumping foliage eventually reaches 2.5 ft_ tall & 3 ft_ wide. Broad gracefully upward-arching 6 wide medium-green leaves have a distinctive creamy-white margin with small brownish-black teeth and terminal spine that add additional visual interest. With age appears a 4-6 flower spike bearing red-tinged green flowers clustered atop the upper third of the flower spike.



Family


Agavaceae



Origin



Mexico (North America)



Care Instructions



  • Keep in a bright sunny location.

  • Try not to water the leaves, give water to soil only.

  • Avoid overwatering as the roots may get rot.

  • You should remove dead, infected or damaged plant parts.

  • Water the plant in a morning, between 8 to 10 am.

  • Avoid keeping in suffocated areas or in dark room.

  • While, re-potting or propagating try not to disturb the roots.


Agave Celsii 'Multicolour' Care



  • Check the moisture in the soil before watering it.

  • Poke your finger into the soil, if dry then apply water.

  • Require natural direct light.

  • Do not re-pot for min. 2 weeks after receiving it.
